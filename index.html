About Me

Business intelligence and systems analyst with experience in developing, documenting, and delivering process improvements and projects aligned with business initiatives.
Consistently provides innovative solutions that facilitate the attainment of business goals.

I have experience in utilizing agile methods for managing projects and programs as well as the software development lifecycle (SDLC).

Much of my business analysis experience falls in the manufacturing sector which has provided me additional training in lean principles. My passion is data. The data of a company is extremely valuable. An organization's success or failure is impacted by its ability to utilize the data it has to make well-informed decisions on business strategies and goals. It is the data managed in an enterprise resource planning (ERP) system and other systems that is stored in relational databases that I work in often. I extract that data using SQL queries into reporting tools like Excel and Microsoft Power BI and then transform the raw data into clear, concise reporting and visualizations for the business users consumption. I have also worked extensively with business users to understand the possible functionality not currently being used of an ERP system to improve processes.
